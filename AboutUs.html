About Us

Tom Export LLC , is a successful consulting company and a recognized partner in outsourcing advisory.
The company has been founded in 2015 by team of specialists and  experienced consultants in Russia.

Since its inception, Tom Export LLC has focused on enhancing the productivity and proﬁtability of its clients. We serve as an extension of each client's management group, providing opportunities to recognize trends and set direction that will lead to the attainment of each ﬁrm's respective goals and the on-going achievement of targeted results.

We provide tailor-made solutions as a result of a blend of advanced skills, professional consultancy and international expertise.

Tom Export LLC is an umbrella corporation promoting activities of a group of companies from the real estate, engineering (civil and special) sector and pharmaceutical & food supplements production.

We direct all our time and effort to business and investment opportunities in attractive industries with growth potential.

We help our clients and partners to achieve strategic and ﬁnancial goals through our detailed know-how and a wide network of partners worldwide. We can provide a wide range of tailor made services based on individual needs.

We are active in selected markets in Europe, Middle East and GCC countries.
